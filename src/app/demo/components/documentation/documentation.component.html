How to get started?

It's really easy!:

  • Get Started: Start by navigating to the "Transcriptions & Notes" page on the hideable menu on the left. Here, you can upload audio files to transcribe audio content seamlessly.
  • Transcriptions & Notes: To begin transcribing, simply upload a file. Our advanced AI technology will handle the rest, providing accurate transcriptions and summaries in no time.

TonalityWEB is mobile-friendly and perfect for busy professionals:

  • Mobile Support: Access TonalityWEB from your smartphone's web browser anytime, anywhere.
  • Record Meetings: Easily record important meetings directly from your phone with just a click.
  • Effortless Transcription: Upload recorded meeting audio files to TonalityWEB for seamless transcription.
  • AI-Powered Summaries: Get concise summaries of meeting discussions using advanced AI technology.
  • Ask Questions: Review transcribed meeting content and ask questions for further clarification.

Transcribe and summarize YouTube videos effortlessly!:

  • Transcribe & Summarize: Navigate to the "Remote URL Transcriptions & Notes" page on the hideable menu on the left. Here, you can provide YouTube URLs to transcribe and summarize video content with ease.

With AI technology, TonalityWEB can summarize transcriptions and answer user questions about the results:

  • Summarize Transcriptions: TonalityWEB utilizes advanced AI technology to generate concise summaries of transcriptions, providing key insights at a glance.
  • Answer User Questions: Users can ask questions about the transcribed content, and TonalityWEB's AI capabilities will provide accurate responses based on the transcriptions.

Tailor your experience by customizing your user preferences in the profile page. Personalize your settings to suit your needs and preferences.

  • Change Theme: Customize the look and feel of TonalityWEB by selecting different themes. Choose from a variety of color palettes to suit your preferences.
  • Light/Dark Mode: Switch between light and dark modes for optimal viewing comfort. Enjoy a personalized viewing experience that matches your environment.
